About Us

Our Mission

At Swap Crypto, Rescue Snowden!, we are dedicated to providing a seamless and secure platform for swapping cryptocurrencies. Our mission is to empower users to trade their favorite tokens while supporting a noble cause: raising awareness and funds to help Edward Snowden.

Who We Are

We are a team of blockchain enthusiasts, developers, and activists who believe in the power of decentralized finance (DeFi) to make a positive impact. Our platform is built on transparency, security, and community-driven values.

How It Works

Our KRC20 Swap Tool allows you to easily swap between popular tokens like KAS, KSDOG, KANGO, NACHO, and USDC. Every swap contributes to our cause, and users have the chance to win exciting prizes like SOL, KAS, or SNOWDN tokens.

Join the Movement

By using our platform, you're not just swapping tokens—you're joining a movement to support freedom of information and privacy rights. Together, we can make a difference.
